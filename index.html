About

Bubblers are the collection of randomly generated NFTs of the Etherium blockchain. The 1st generation consists of 8,888 arts randomly assembled from over 300 traits.

Bubblers have a variety of outfits, backgrounds and colours. Each Bubblers is comprised of a unique body, head, background, outfits & accessories.

Bubblers is part of one big Metaverse which we will to develop with our community.

Bubblers can be everywhere. You can find them in various other collections in other styles. Everything Bubbler touches will become more valuable and in demand!

frequently asked questions

How much to mint a Bubbler?
Bubbler will be listed at 0.088 ETH per mint.
How many will there be?
8888 Bubblers will be created and minted. 50 mints will be reserved for our team and for the development of our community.
How Mintpasses work and how much do they cost?
You will be able to mint 1 Mintpass per one person. You will have 24 hours тbefore the start of the sale to mint your Bubblers. It cost 0,1 ETH. You can find more information about all Mintpass privileges in the ‘Mintpass’ section.
What is Bubbleron?
It’s the first NFT social network. We have big plans for Bubbleron. For now we have a [beta version](Bubbleron). Try it!
